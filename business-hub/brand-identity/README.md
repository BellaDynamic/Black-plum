## Current Brand Standards

### Brand Voice

**Tone:** Professional, approachable, expert, and transformative  
**Language:** Clear, direct, and accessibleâ€”avoiding overly ornate phrasing  
**Focus:** Multi-industry expertise, personalized consulting, fulfillment for founders

### Prohibited Language

- "Gothic luxury" or "gothic elegance"
- "Dark elegance"
- Overly ornate or archaic phrasing
- The word "paramount" (trademark conflict)
- Any language that creates distance between the founder and the client

**Primary Tagline:** "Your Direct Portal to Brandy Bianchini"

**Supporting Messages:**
- "Multi-industry advising and consulting applied to your individual needs"
- "Fulfillment for founders and projects at any phase, any stage"
- "Transformation Through Excellence"

### Visual Identity

**Color Palette:**
- Black: #1a0f1b
- Plum: #9B4F96
- Gold: #d4af37
- Cream: #F5F1ED
- Silver: #C0C0C0

**Typography:**
- Body: Georgia serif with generous letter-spacing
- Headings: Playfair Display

**Aesthetic:** Professional excellence, rarity and unique foundational legacy, lasting generation
