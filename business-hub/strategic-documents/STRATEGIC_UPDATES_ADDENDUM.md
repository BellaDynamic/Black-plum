### 5. Apparel Sourcing Standards

**New Standard:** All staff clothing and uniforms must be sourced from the US, explicitly excluding items from China or Korea.

**Fit Profile for Founder/Lead Staff:**
- Height: 5'8"
- Weight: 195 lbs
- Build: Broad shoulders, 40" bust, long arms
- Fit: Tailored, slim, and slimming, with ample room in the sleeves and longer length extending to lower hips
- Style Elements: Slight puff in shoulders, elegant and classy aesthetic
- Preferred Material: Black, soft ski leather, in zip-up or button-up style

**Rationale:** Establishing clear sourcing and fit standards ensures brand consistency and quality control for all staff apparel.
