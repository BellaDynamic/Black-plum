## GRID Scorecards: The Showcase Tool for Opportunity and Network Building

The GRID (Global Responsible Integrated Development Tool) is your proprietary system for demonstrating the scope, scale, and opportunity of your ecosystem. The GRID scorecards serve as both an internal management tool and an external showcase for partners, investors, and collaborators.

### GRID Structure

**Minimum:** 16 cells per state  
**Current Status:** Grid filled two-fold with internal assets and entities (32+ cells minimum)  
**Function:** Each cell can hold a project, event, activity, brand, or business

### GRID Scorecard Components

**For Each Geographic Region (State/Province/Country):**

1. **Asset Deployment Map**
   - Which brands are active in this region
   - Which programs are deployed
   - Which partnerships are established
   - Which revenue streams are active

2. **Opportunity Identification**
   - Surplus government property procurement opportunities
   - Economic development partnerships
   - Foundation and grant opportunities
   - Corporate partnership potential

3. **Network Building Metrics**
   - Number of service providers recruited
   - Number of volunteers engaged
   - Number of clients served
   - Number of institutional partnerships

4. **Cash Flow Tracking**
   - Revenue by brand in this region
   - Revenue by service type
   - Revenue by client tier
   - Projected growth trajectory

5. **Impact Metrics**
   - Lives transformed
   - Businesses supported
   - Crises resolved
   - Community resources deployed

### GRID Scorecard Use Cases

**Internal Management:**
- Track deployment of all four brands across jurisdictions
- Identify gaps and opportunities for expansion
- Monitor cash flow and revenue by region
- Coordinate volunteer and service provider networks

**External Showcase:**
- Demonstrate scope and scale to potential partners
- Show institutional investors the opportunity
- Recruit service providers with clear deployment plan
- Attract foundation and government funding with impact data

**Strategic Planning:**
- Identify priority regions for expansion
- Allocate resources based on opportunity and capacity
- Coordinate events and programs across regions
- Build network effects and referral systems
