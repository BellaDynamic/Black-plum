# Luna's Garden – Tier 2 Program Overview

**Date:** March 04, 2026  
**Purpose:** Sanctuary and platform for producing, promoting, and nurturing diverse voices, sounds, art, and expression.  
**Tagline:** "Everything grows in my garden."

---

## 1. Core Mission & Purpose

Luna's Garden is the creative sanctuary within the Eternal Autumn Ecosystem where passion meets purpose and is professionally placed. It serves as the incubator for artists, sound engineers, producers, and healing creatives, providing pathways from creation to production to distribution.

---

## 2. Key Program Elements

### 2.1. Music & Promotions Plan
- **Artist Showcases:** Curated performances and talent exhibitions.
- **Indie Label Alignments:** Strategic partnerships with independent music labels.
- **Streaming Platform Integration:** Distribution across major streaming platforms.
- **Performance & Tour Coordination:** Professional booking and tour management support.

### 2.2. Talent Placement Pipeline
- **Internal Auditions & Evaluations:** Rigorous assessment of artistic potential.
- **Artist Management & Coaching:** Personal growth and career development support.
- **Member-to-Mentor Matching:** Peer learning and mentorship pathways.
- **Residency & Production Opportunities:** Access to professional production facilities and creative residencies.
