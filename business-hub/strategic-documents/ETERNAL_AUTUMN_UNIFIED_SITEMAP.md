# Eternal Autumn: Unified Ecosystem Sitemap & Navigation Plan

**Date:** March 04, 2026  
**Status:** Strategic Design Phase  
**Goal:** Unify all siloed elements under the "Eternal Autumn" primary brand identity.

---

## 1. Core Brand Architecture

The entire digital property is now unified under the **Eternal Autumn** identity. The hierarchy is as follows:

- **Primary Brand:** Eternal Autumn (The Ecosystem)
- **Foundational Entity:** Eternal Autumn Trust (Private Operating Foundation)
- **Operational Engine:** Universal Changemakers Series L3C
- **Direct Portal:** Black Plum Concierge (A Division of Eternal Autumn)
- **Sister Brand:** Bella Dynamic (Wellness Advocacy within Eternal Autumn)

---

## 2. Unified Navigation Structure (Global)

## 3. Page-by-Page Curation Plan

### 3.1. Homepage (`index.html`) - *The Gateway to Eternal Autumn*
- **Hero Section:** Rebranded to feature "Eternal Autumn" as the primary headline. Universal Changemakers Series L3C remains the secondary badge.
- **Ecosystem Section:** Redesigned to show Eternal Autumn as the "Whole" with Black Plum and Bella Dynamic as the "Parts."
- **Mission:** Integrated "Eternal Autumn" philosophy (generational wealth, dynasty formation, and "being there for one another").
- **Aesthetic:** Shift to the sophisticated "Deep Plum/Black" palette globally.

### 3.2. BWEN Hub (`bwen_hub.html`) - *The Content Engine*
- Fully integrated into the global navigation.
- Linked directly from the "Creative & Design" pillar of Black Plum.
- Showcases original shows like "Specters and Secrets Radio."

### 3.3. Black Plum Concierge (`black_plum_concierge.html`) - *The Service Portal*
- Rebranded as "Black Plum: An Eternal Autumn Division."
- Links directly to Agritourism events and BWEN content.
- Integrates the RAPID framework and Beneflts platform.

### 3.4. Agritourism & Events (`agritourism_events.html`) - *The Experience Hub*
- Features the "Ball of the Macabre" as the flagship event.
- Connects harvest partners with the Black Plum Concierge service.
- Linked from the "Travel & Events" pillar.

## 4. Aesthetic & Visual Curation

- **Colors:** Primary use of **Deep Plum (#1a0f1b)** and **Black**. Accents in **Lavender (#9b7fd4)** and **Deep Teal (#007a7a)**.
- **Typography:** **Playfair Display** for headings (elegance/rarity) and **Inter/Georgia** for body (professionalism).
- **Integrity:** Explicitly avoid "gothic" descriptors while maintaining the atmospheric richness of the autumn season. Focus on "rarity," "foundational legacy," and "lasting generation."

---

## 5. Strategic Interconnections (The "Cogs")

1.  **BWEN → Events:** BWEN films and documents all Agritourism and Masquerade events.
2.  **Events → Concierge:** Events serve as the entry point for high-touch Black Plum Concierge services.
3.  **Concierge → Trust:** All services fuel the Eternal Autumn Trust's mission of generational wealth and community impact.
4.  **Trust → Programs:** The Trust funds the Universal Changemakers programs (F.U.S.E.D) which are PRI/DAF ready.
