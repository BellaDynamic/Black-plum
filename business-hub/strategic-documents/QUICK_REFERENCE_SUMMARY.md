# Black Plum: Quick Reference Summary

**One-Page Strategic Overview**

---

## Core Identity

**Black Plum Concierge** is the direct portal to founder **Brandy Bianchini** and the public-facing brand for **Universal Changemakers (L3C)**. It provides transformative, multi-industry advising and consulting across six service pillars. The organization operates through a Low-Profit Limited Liability Company structure, supported by the **Eternal Autumn Trust** (private operating foundation).

**Mission:** Resolve systemic challenges in business management, finance, and law while providing fulfillment for founders and projects at any lifecycle stage.

**Ultimate Function:** Movement of people and money to push resources back into the community and affect **"bottom line changes threefold."**

**Geographic Scope:** U.S., Mexico, Canada, U.K., E.U.

## Program Requirements

- **Four core programs minimum** (managed by Better World)
- **Four events/projects per program per year** (16 total annually)
- **100% PRI and DAF ready** (Program-Related Investment & Donor Advised Fund)
- **Public and private-facing components** required
- **Monthly content creation** tied to production schedule
