Each month will feature a comprehensive forecast delivered across multiple platforms and formats, creating a cohesive content ecosystem that serves different audience preferences.

**Week 2: Deep Dive - Health & Wealth**

## Content Production System: Batch Creation for Efficiency

To avoid being pulled in too many directions and to protect your energy, you need a batch content creation system that allows you to produce a month's worth of content in 1-2 days.

### Monthly Batch Day (6-8 hours)

**Setup (30 minutes):**
- Prepare recording space (lighting, camera, microphone)
- Review monthly forecast outline and talking points
- Set up streaming software and recording settings
- Test all equipment

**Recording Session 1: Monthly Forecast (90 minutes):**
- Record YouTube Live Event (or pre-record for later streaming)
- Cover all five pillars: health, wealth, love, leadership, resources
- Include Q&A segment (pre-submitted questions or anticipated FAQs)
- Calls to action for programs, services, and contribution platforms

**Break (15 minutes)**

**Recording Session 2: Weekly Deep Dives (90 minutes):**
- Record 4 weekly videos (15-20 minutes each)
- Week 2: Health & Wealth Deep Dive
- Week 3: Love & Leadership Deep Dive
- Week 4: Integration & Q&A
- Bonus: Patreon-exclusive content

**Break (15 minutes)**

**Recording Session 3: Shorts and Clips (60 minutes):**
- Record 10-15 YouTube Shorts (60-90 seconds each)
- Key insights, quotes, tips from the monthly forecast
- Promotional clips for live events and programs

**Writing Session: Substack Articles (90-120 minutes):**
- Write monthly forecast article (2,000-3,000 words)
- Outline weekly Substack posts (1,000-1,500 words each)
- Create templates and worksheets for paid subscribers

**Social Media Planning (30 minutes):**
- Schedule posts for the month (Buffer, Hootsuite, or Later)
- Create graphics and captions for each post
- Plan cross-promotion across all platforms

### Delegation for Content Production

**Content Editor (15-20 hours/month):**
- Edit all video and audio content
- Create YouTube Shorts from longer videos
- Upload and schedule content across platforms
- Design thumbnails and graphics
- Manage social media scheduling

**Virtual Assistant (10 hours/month):**
- Upload podcast episodes to hosting platform
- Publish Substack articles
- Update Patreon with exclusive content
- Respond to comments and messages
- Track analytics and engagement

**Total Delegation Cost:** $1,500-$2,500/month  
**Your Time Investment:** 6-8 hours/month for content creation + 4-6 hours/month for live Q&A and community engagement
