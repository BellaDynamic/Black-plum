## Key Features and Innovations

### 1. GRID Integration
Every timeline is mapped into the GRID system with status indicators:
- **Pre-GRID:** Discernment phase (Gray)
- **GRID Cell Reserved:** Assess/Pre-Gab (Yellow)
- **GRID Cell Active:** Assess/Gab through Assess/RF-FS (Blue)
- **GRID Cell Launch Ready:** Pre-Launch (Orange)
- **GRID Cell Executing:** Planned Phase (Green)

### 2. Behind-the-Scenes Documentation
Each phase includes detailed documentation of:
- Internal activities (cohort member work)
- Organizational activities (support and coordination)
- Deliverables and milestones
- Transition criteria

### 3. Year-to-Year Timeline Structure
Formal timelines show:
- Complete journey from Discernment to Year 2 execution
- Quarterly (Per Q) breakdowns with specific activities
- Integration with newsletter, video, events, and sponsorships
- Alignment with organizational programs and initiatives

### 4. Hard Assets for Presentation
All documentation and visuals are designed as **formal, hard assets** that can be:
- Presented to cohort members for onboarding
- Used in external stakeholder presentations
- Referenced in service agreements and contracts
- Updated and maintained as living documents
