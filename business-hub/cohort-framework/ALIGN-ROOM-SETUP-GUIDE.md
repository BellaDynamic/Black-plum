### Step 3: Schedule Discovery Calls

*   **Action:** Follow up the initial message by scheduling 15-20 minute discovery calls.
*   **Priority Order:**
    1.  Super Circus
    2.  Key Venues (NASCAR, NHRA)
    3.  Major Sponsors

### Step 4: Manage Contracts

*   **Action:** Use Align to distribute, negotiate, and obtain signatures on all contracts.
*   **Process:**
    1.  Send the appropriate contract template to the stakeholder.
    2.  Use Align's features to discuss and redline any changes.
    3.  Once finalized, use a digital signature tool (like the one integrated with Beneflts) to execute the agreement.

## 3. BEST PRACTICES

*   **Centralize Communication:** Keep all project-related conversations within Align to maintain a single source of truth.
*   **Document Everything:** Use Align to log meeting notes, decisions, and verbal commitments.
*   **Use Task Boards:** Track the progress of the 30-day action plan and assign tasks to ensure accountability.
*   **Maintain Professionalism:** All communications should be clear, concise, and professional, reflecting the structured nature of the project.
