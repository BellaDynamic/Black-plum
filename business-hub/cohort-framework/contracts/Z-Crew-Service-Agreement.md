## 1. SCOPE OF WORK

Z Crew agrees to provide the following content capture services (the "Services") for the events and locations listed in the attached **Statement of Work (SOW)**:

*   **Primary Cinematography:** Capture high-quality video footage of all designated events, interviews, and behind-the-scenes activities.
*   **Audio Recording:** Ensure professional-grade audio is captured for all scenes and interviews.
*   **B-Roll Capture:** Film supplementary footage to be used for cutaways and establishing shots.
*   **Data Management:** Securely manage and back up all captured footage on-site and deliver to Producer.
*   **Coordination:** Work closely with the Producer and Event Crew to capture key moments without disrupting live events.
