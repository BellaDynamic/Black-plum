## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Operator agrees to provide a complete, turnkey Super Circus event (the "Services") at various locations as part of the Project. The Services shall include, but are not limited to:

*   **Full Event Production:** All performers, acts, and equipment required for the Super Circus show.
*   **Logistics:** Transportation, setup (load-in), and teardown (load-out) of all circus equipment.
*   **Staffing:** All necessary personnel to run the circus, including performers, technicians, and front-of-house staff.
*   **Concessions:** Management of all food, beverage, and merchandise sales at the event.

---

## 2. STATE-BY-STATE OPERATIONS

This Agreement shall govern all Super Circus events for the Project across the United States. A separate **Statement of Work (SOW)** will be executed for each state, which will be incorporated into this Agreement by reference.

Each SOW will define:
*   **Event Location(s):** Specific venues and cities within the state.
*   **Event Dates:** The schedule for all performances.
*   **Footprint/Space Requirements:** The exact physical space needed for the circus tent, vehicles, and operations.
*   **Local Permitting:** Responsibilities for securing all necessary local and state permits.
