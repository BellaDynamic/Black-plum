# Resources

This directory contains templates, tools, and reference materials for team use across all Black Plum operations.

## Resource Categories

### Contract Templates
Jurisdiction-specific contract templates for all service agreements.

**Contract Types:**
- Consultation Agreement
- Membership Agreement
- Project Services Agreement
- Advocacy Partnership Agreement
- Confidentiality & NDA

**Jurisdictions:**
- United States (Utah, California, New York, Texas, Florida)
- Canada (Ontario, British Columbia)
- United Kingdom (England, Scotland)
- European Union (Germany, France, Netherlands)
- Mexico

### Industry-Specific Tools
Tailored resources for each target industry sector.

**Industries:**
- Technology & SaaS
- Real Estate & Development
- Healthcare & Wellness
- Creative & Agency Services
- Professional Services (Legal, Accounting, Consulting)

### Financial Planning Worksheets
Tools for client financial capacity assessment and service alignment.

**Tools:**
- Cost of Living Assessment
- Cost of Business Assessment
- Combined Financial Profile
- Service Trigger Matrix
- Phased Implementation Tracker

### Training Materials
Internal training resources for team development and onboarding.

**Materials:**
- Sales methodology training (emotional triggers, problem awareness questioning)
- Brand voice and messaging training
- Operational procedures training
- Field agent onboarding
- Client service standards

### Staff Development
Resources for staff appearance, apparel, and professional standards.

**Materials:**
- Staff clothing line specifications (Pietra Studio partnership)
- Apparel sourcing standards (US-sourced, specific fit requirements)
- Brand standards for staff appearance
- Client interaction protocols
