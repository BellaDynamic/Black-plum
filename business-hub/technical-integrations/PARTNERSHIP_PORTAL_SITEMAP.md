## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the sitemap and navigation architecture for the Universal Changemakers Partnership Portal. The portal will serve as a centralized hub for integrating and managing services from our key strategic partners. Its primary goal is to create a seamless, unified experience for both internal teams and external clients, streamlining workflows and enhancing service delivery across the entire ecosystem.

The portal will provide a single point of access to a suite of powerful tools, enabling us to deliver on our core mission of providing holistic, transformative solutions.

---

## 2. High-Level Sitemap

The portal will be organized into the following top-level sections:

- **/ (Dashboard):** A personalized landing page providing a high-level overview of all integrated services, recent activity, and key metrics.
- **/partners:** A directory of all integrated partners, with detailed information on each platform and its capabilities.
- **/integrations:** A management hub for connecting, configuring, and monitoring all active partner integrations.
- **/resources:** A library of documentation, training materials, and best practices for leveraging the integrated ecosystem.
- **/support:** A dedicated support center with FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and a contact point for assistance.
- **/settings:** User profile management, notification preferences, and account settings.

---

## 3. Detailed Sitemap & Partner Integrations

### 3.1. / (Dashboard)

- **My Workspace:** A customizable view of the user's most frequently used tools and services.
- **Recent Activity:** A feed of recent actions, updates, and notifications from all integrated platforms.
- **Key Metrics:** High-level analytics and reporting on client engagement, project status, and financial performance.

### 3.2. /partners

This section will feature a dedicated page for each integrated partner, providing a comprehensive overview of their services and our integration.

| Partner | Description | Key Integration Points |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sophia Health** | Functional medicine platform | Client health data, lab results, and wellness plans |
| **Rupa Health** | Specialty lab testing service | Ordering and tracking lab tests, viewing results |
| **Practice Panther** | Legal practice management | Case management, document storage, billing |
| **CaseWorthy** | Human services case management | Client intake, needs assessment, and program management |
| **Odoo** | All-in-one business management software | CRM, accounting, project management, and ERP |
| **Connecteam** | Employee management app | Team communication, scheduling, and task management |
| **Betterworld** | Fundraising and donor management | Campaign tracking, donation processing, and donor engagement |

### 3.3. /integrations

- **/integrations/manage:** A central dashboard for managing all active integrations. Users can connect new services, configure settings, and monitor the status of each connection.
- **/integrations/api-keys:** A secure repository for managing API keys and other credentials.
- **/integrations/logs:** Detailed logs of all API calls and data synchronization events for troubleshooting and auditing purposes.

### 3.4. /resources

- **/resources/documentation:** Comprehensive guides for each integrated platform and the portal itself.
- **/resources/training:** A library of video tutorials, webinars, and training modules.
- **/resources/best-practices:** A collection of articles and case studies on how to best leverage the integrated ecosystem.

### 3.5. /support

- **/support/faq:** A searchable database of frequently asked questions.
- **/support/troubleshooting:** Step-by-step guides for resolving common issues.
- **/support/contact:** A form for submitting support tickets and contacting the portal administration team.

### 3.6. /settings

- **/settings/profile:** Manage personal information, contact details, and password.
- **/settings/notifications:** Configure email and in-app notification preferences.
- **/settings/team:** (For administrators) Manage user accounts, roles, and permissions.

## 4. Navigation Architecture

The portal will feature a persistent, left-hand sidebar navigation menu, providing easy access to all top-level sections. The navigation will be designed to be intuitive and consistent across the entire platform.

- **Primary Navigation (Sidebar):**
  - Dashboard
  - Partners
  - Integrations
  - Resources
  - Support

- **Secondary Navigation (Header):**
  - User Profile / Settings
  - Notifications
  - Search Bar

This structure will ensure that users can easily find the information and tools they need, regardless of where they are in the portal. The design will be clean, modern, and fully responsive, providing a seamless experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
