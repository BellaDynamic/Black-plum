# Programs

This directory contains documentation for the four core programs under Better World, including PRI/DAF materials, event schedules, and impact measurement frameworks.

## Program Requirements

All programs must meet the following criteria:

- **100% PRI (Program-Related Investment) and DAF (Donor Advised Fund) Ready**
- **Minimum of four events/projects per year per program** (16 total annually)
- **Both public-facing and private-facing components**
- **Triggered by cause and needs arena**
- **Accessed via membership first**

## Recommended Program Structure

### Program 1: Health & Wellness Transformation
**Focus:** Systemic health services improvement  
**Target:** Healthcare providers, wellness practitioners, community health organizations  
**Events:** Quarterly workshops, annual conference, monthly webinars

### Program 2: Financial Empowerment & Economic Mobility
**Focus:** Financial literacy, business sustainability, economic development  
**Target:** Entrepreneurs, small business owners, underserved communities  
**Events:** Quarterly training series, annual summit, monthly coaching sessions

### Program 3: Creative Economy & Artist Development
**Focus:** Music industry support, creative entrepreneurship, entertainment platform development  
**Target:** Artists, creatives, entertainment professionals  
**Events:** Quarterly showcases, annual festival, monthly artist circles

### Program 4: Community Infrastructure & Systems Innovation
**Focus:** Organizational development, systems design, process optimization  
**Target:** Nonprofits, social enterprises, community organizations  
**Events:** Quarterly consulting intensives, annual innovation lab, monthly office hours
