# Black Plum Concierge Agency - Operational Infrastructure

## Intake & Needs Assessment System

### Phase 1: Initial Contact (RingCentral)

**Call Flow:**
1. Client calls Black Plum number (routed through RingCentral)
2. Automated system identifies caller timezone and routes to appropriate field agent
3. Initial greeting and service inquiry capture
4. Scheduling of comprehensive intake assessment

**Information Captured:**
- Name, contact information, timezone
- Primary service interest (Creative, Business, Travel, Lifestyle, Coaching, Infrastructure)
- Urgency level (Immediate, 30 days, 90 days, planning phase)
- Preliminary financial comfort level

### Phase 2: Comprehensive Intake Assessment

**Financial Capacity Analysis:**

1. **Cost of Living Assessment**
   - Monthly housing costs
   - Essential living expenses
   - Current debt obligations
   - Emergency fund status
   - Dependents/family obligations

2. **Cost of Business Assessment** (if applicable)
   - Business revenue (annual/monthly)
   - Operating expenses
   - Payroll obligations
   - Equipment/infrastructure costs
   - Growth capital needs

3. **Combined Financial Profile**
   - Total monthly income (employment + business)
   - Total monthly obligations
   - Available discretionary income
   - Investment capacity for services
   - Payment structure preferences (monthly, quarterly, project-based)

**Service Scope Determination:**

1. **Frequency Assessment**
   - One-time consultation
   - Monthly ongoing support
   - Quarterly strategic planning
   - Weekly tactical support
   - Ad-hoc as-needed basis

2. **Urgency & Scope**
   - Critical/immediate need
   - High priority (next 30 days)
   - Medium priority (next 90 days)
   - Planning phase (6+ months)
   - Scope: Single service vs. integrated multi-service

3. **Dual Pathway Identification**
   - **Solo Entrepreneur Mode:** Self-employed, business owner, freelancer
   - **Professional Mode:** W-2 employment, corporate role, organizational position
   - **Hybrid Mode:** Both income streams active
   - Service needs differ by pathway

### Phase 3: Service Pathway Identification

**Service Trigger Matrix:**

| Financial Tier | Service Recommendation | Frequency | Delivery Model |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Minimal** ($250-500/mo available) | Consultation-based (hourly) | Monthly or quarterly | Virtual, group coaching |
| **Moderate** ($500-1,500/mo available) | Standard Membership | Monthly | Virtual + limited in-person |
| **Comfortable** ($1,500-3,000/mo available) | Premium Membership | Bi-weekly or monthly | Hybrid delivery |
| **Affluent** ($3,000+/mo available) | Elite Membership | Weekly or as-needed | Dedicated support, in-person |

**Service Add-On Triggers:**

- **Creative & Design:** Activated when brand/visual refresh needed, new business launch, or rebranding initiative
- **Business Development:** Activated when revenue growth, funding, or partnership expansion is priority
- **Travel & Events:** Activated when retreat, conference, or major event coordination needed
- **Lifestyle Services:** Activated when personal/family management, real estate, or wellness focus emerges
- **Coaching & Counsel:** Activated when leadership development, organizational challenges, or strategic decisions needed
- **Infrastructure & Systems:** Activated when process optimization, automation, or system design needed

### Phase 4: Phased Service Implementation

**Earned & Raised Model:**

1. **Month 1-3: Foundation Phase**
   - Core membership service active
   - Initial consultation and assessment complete
   - One primary service area engaged
   - Financial baseline established

2. **Month 4-6: Integration Phase**
   - Evaluate results and impact from foundation services
   - Identify secondary service needs
   - Add complementary services as capacity allows
   - Adjust frequency based on results

3. **Month 7-12: Expansion Phase**
   - Integrate additional service areas
   - Increase frequency or depth as financial capacity grows
   - Develop long-term strategic roadmap
   - Establish ongoing support structure

4. **Year 2+: Optimization Phase**
   - Full service ecosystem engaged
   - Continuous optimization and refinement
   - Proactive service triggers based on business/life changes
   - Advocacy and referral pathway activation

## RFP & Project Management

### RFP Process

1. **RFP Submission**
   - Client submits detailed project brief
   - Scope, timeline, budget parameters defined
   - Industry and jurisdiction specified

2. **Initial Assessment**
   - Black Plum team reviews fit and feasibility
   - Resource availability confirmed
   - Preliminary timeline and approach outlined

3. **Proposal Development**
   - Detailed scope of work
   - Team composition and expertise
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Investment and payment terms
   - Success metrics and deliverables

4. **Presentation & Negotiation**
   - Proposal presentation to client
   - Q&A and clarification
   - Contract negotiation
   - Final approval and kickoff

### Project Delivery Framework

**RAPID Phase Planning Model:**

- **R - Research & Requirements** - Comprehensive discovery and analysis
- **A - Architecture & Alignment** - System design and stakeholder alignment
- **P - Planning & Preparation** - Detailed planning and resource allocation
- **I - Implementation & Integration** - Execution and integration
- **D - Delivery & Documentation** - Final delivery and knowledge transfer

## House Rules & Guardrails

### Client Expectations

1. **Communication Standards**
   - Response time: 24 hours for standard inquiries
   - Emergency support: 4 hours for urgent matters (Elite members)
   - Communication channel preferences respected
   - Timezone considerations honored

2. **Confidentiality & Privacy**
   - All client information strictly confidential
   - NDA signed before sensitive discussions
   - No public reference without written permission
   - Data security and GDPR compliance maintained

3. **Engagement Standards**
   - Clear scope and deliverables defined upfront
   - Regular progress updates and check-ins
   - Transparent communication about challenges
   - Collaborative problem-solving approach

### Agency Standards

1. **Quality Assurance**
   - All work meets defined quality standards
   - Regular review and feedback cycles
   - Continuous improvement mindset
   - Industry best practices applied

2. **Ethical Standards**
   - Integrity-first decision making
   - No conflicts of interest
   - Transparent about capabilities and limitations
   - Honest assessment of fit and feasibility

3. **Professional Development**
   - Continuous learning and skill development
   - Industry certifications and credentials
   - Best practices and methodology updates
   - Peer collaboration and knowledge sharing
